About Our Organization

The College of Engineering and Architecture (CEA) at Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges is dedicated to fostering a collaborative and innovative environment where students can thrive. Our organization encompasses a variety of student groups, professional societies, and extracurricular activities that promote personal and professional growth.
